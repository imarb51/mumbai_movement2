About MMA

Where passion for movement meets excellence in training

The MMA Philosophy

"Be Strong To Be Useful" — Our founding principle

Why MMA Exists

Movement is more than physical training — it's a mindset. Founded in 2019, Mumbai Movement Academy started with a simple belief: everyone deserves to discover the power of movement. What began as a small parkour class in Andheri has grown into Mumbai's premier movement academy, with five locations and a community of over 1,000 active members.

We've trained kids, teens, adults, corporate teams, and even Indian Army personnel. Our mission is to make movement accessible, safe, and transformative for everyone.

Progression

Every skill builds on the last. We never rush. Safety and technique come first. Our structured level system ensures steady, sustainable growth.

Community

Train together, grow together. Our community supports every member's journey. We celebrate wins, learn from challenges, and push each other forward.

Expression

Find your unique style. Movement is art, and you are the artist. We encourage creativity and personal expression in every training session.

Community & Culture

At MMA, you're not just joining a gym — you're becoming part of a movement. Our community spans ages, backgrounds, and abilities, united by a shared passion for learning and growth.

We host regular events, workshops, community sessions, and celebrations that bring our members together beyond just training. This is where friendships form, skills are shared, and the joy of movement is celebrated.

Meet Our Founders

The visionaries behind Mumbai Movement Academy

Dr. Rishi Prasad

Co-Founder & Head Coach

Dr. Rishi Prasad is a certified sports physiotherapist with over 15 years of experience in movement science. He pioneered the integration of parkour with rehabilitation techniques, creating a unique training methodology that emphasizes injury prevention and functional fitness.

Under his guidance, Mumbai Movement Academy has become a leading institution for movement education in India. His philosophy centers on making movement accessible to everyone, regardless of age or fitness level.

Certifications: Sports Physiotherapy, Parkour Coaching Level 3, Movement & Mobility Specialist

Cyrus Khan

Co-Founder & Movement Director

Cyrus Khan is one of India's first professional parkour athletes and has represented the country at international competitions. With expertise in freerunning, calisthenics, and stunt coordination, he brings a creative and dynamic approach to movement training.

He has worked with major brands like Red Bull and Nike, and has coached elite performers including Bollywood stunt teams and Indian Army Special Forces. His innovative training methods blend athleticism with artistry.

Achievements: International Parkour Champion, Red Bull Athlete, Bollywood Stunt Coordinator

Krishna Mehta

Co-Founder & Business Development

Krishna Mehta brings her MBA and corporate strategy background to scale Mumbai Movement Academy from a single studio to five locations across Mumbai. Her vision extends beyond just training facilities — she's building a movement culture.

She has established partnerships with schools, universities, and corporate organizations, making movement training accessible to thousands of individuals across different demographics. Her leadership has been instrumental in MMA's growth and community impact.

Expertise: Business Strategy, Partnership Development, Community Building
