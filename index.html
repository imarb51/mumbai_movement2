What We Train

World-class training programs designed to transform how you move

PARKOUR

Parkour at MMA is taught in a controlled, indoor environment with trained coaches. Students learn how to jump, land, climb, and move safely through obstacles using proper technique and body control.

Why parents like it:

  • Improves balance and coordination
  • Builds confidence and problem-solving skills
  • Emphasises safety and control, not stunts

We focus on how to move well, not how to show off.

ROCK CLIMBING

Rock climbing helps develop full-body strength, focus, and patience. All sessions are supervised and use safety equipment suited to the age and level of the student.

What it develops:

  • Upper and lower body strength
  • Grip, balance, and coordination
  • Mental focus and planning

CALISTHENICS

Calisthenics is strength training using your own body weight. Classes focus on building strength, mobility, and control in a gradual and beginner-friendly way.

Benefits:

  • No heavy weights involved
  • Progresses at your own pace
  • Suitable for beginners and adults

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our training programs

About Parkour

Parkour is a movement discipline that teaches people how to move efficiently through obstacles using skills like running, jumping, climbing, and balancing. At MMA, it is taught in a structured and controlled environment.

Parkour helps improve strength, balance, coordination, agility, and body awareness. It also supports confidence, focus, problem-solving, and resilience, especially in children.

Yes. Parkour can be adapted for different ages and fitness levels. At MMA, classes are designed specifically for toddlers, kids, teens, and adults, with age-appropriate training and supervision.

Like any physical activity, parkour involves movement and effort. However, when taught properly with supervision, structured progressions, and safety protocols, risks are significantly reduced. Safety is our top priority at MMA.

About Rock Climbing

Rock climbing is a movement activity that involves climbing up a wall using strength, balance, and problem-solving. At MMA, it is taught on indoor climbing walls in a controlled and supervised environment.

Rock climbing helps improve full-body strength, coordination, grip strength, focus, and problem-solving skills. It also builds confidence as climbers learn to trust their movement and make decisions calmly.

When taught correctly, rock climbing is a safe activity. At MMA, all classes are conducted under supervision with safety equipment and controlled progressions. Indoor climbing sessions use crash mats and safety setups appropriate for each student's ability level.

No prior experience is needed. Beginners are welcome, and classes start with basic techniques before progressing gradually.

About Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a form of strength training that uses your own body weight instead of heavy gym equipment. Exercises include movements like push-ups, squats, hanging, and controlled holds, all focused on building strength and control.

Calisthenics helps improve overall strength, mobility and flexibility, balance and coordination, body control and posture. It is a practical and effective way to get stronger without relying on machines or weights.

Yes. Calisthenics at MMA is designed to be beginner-friendly. Exercises are adapted to individual ability, and students progress gradually based on comfort and strength. No prior experience is needed.

Yes. With consistent training and gradual progression, calisthenics can help build noticeable strength and muscle using body weight alone.
